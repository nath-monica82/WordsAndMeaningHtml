V

1)vanity
connotation/emotion:negative
i)meaning:excessive pride in or admiration of one's own appearance or achievements.
example:"it flattered his vanity to think I was in love with him"
synonyms:conceit, conceitedness, self-conceit, narcissism, self-love, self-admiration, self-regard, self-absorption, self-obsession, self-centredness, egotism, egoism, egocentrism, egomania.
antonyms:modesty
ii)meaning:denoting a person or company publishing works at the author's expense.
example:"a vanity press"
iii)meaning:the quality of being worthless or futile.
example:"the vanity of human wishes";"the vanity of all desires of the will".
synonyms:futility, uselessness, pointlessness, worthlessness, purposelessness, idleness, fruitlessness, profitlessness
origin
vanus:empty latin
------------------
vain:egoistic english
----------------------
vanity:selflove;company publishing works of an author;worthless.

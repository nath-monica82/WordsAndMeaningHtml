J

1)jettison connotation/emotion:neutral
i)meaning:throw or drop (something) from an aircraft or ship.
example:"six aircraft jettisoned their loads in the sea"
ii)meaning:abandon or discard (someone or something that is no longer wanted).
"the scheme was jettisoned"
synonyms:dump, drop, ditch, discharge, eject, throw out, empty out, pour out, tip out, unload, throw overboard, throw over the side
antonyms:load, keep, retain.
origin
jactare:to throw latin

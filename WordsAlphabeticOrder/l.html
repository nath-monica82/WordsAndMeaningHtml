1)languish connotation/emotion:negative
i)meaning:(of a person, animal, or plant) lose or lack vitality; grow weak.
example:"plants may appear to be languishing simply because they are dormant"
synonyms:weaken, grow weak, deteriorate, decline, go into a decline.
antonyms:thrive, flourish
ii)meaning:fail to make progress or be successful.
example:"Kelso languish near the bottom of the Scottish First Division"
archaic
iii)meaning:pine(miss) with love or grief.
example:"she still languished after Richard"
archaic
iv)meaning:assume a sentimentally tender or melancholy expression or tone.
example:"when a visitor comes in, she smiles and languishes"
v)meaning:be forced to remain in an unpleasant place or situation.
example:"he has been languishing in jail since 1974"
synonyms:waste away, rot, decay, wither away, moulder, be abandoned, be neglected, be forgotten, suffer.
origin
latin
languish:go on a decline;fail to grow(like a place languishes);miss with love or grief(languish after),be abandoned (languishing in jail)
pine,languish they bothe mean the same.
